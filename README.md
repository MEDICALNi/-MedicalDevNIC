# MedicalDevNIC

MedicalDevNIC is an open-source healthcare platform dedicated to improving healthcare delivery, medical education, and clinical research across Latin America.

## Mission

Our mission is to provide free, accessible, and reliable digital health solutions for physicians, medical students, researchers, and healthcare organizations.

## Current Goals

- AI-assisted clinical decision support
- Medical calculators
- Electronic health records
- Medical education platform
- Clinical research tools
- Telemedicine support

## Technology

- Laravel
- PHP
- MySQL
- JavaScript
- REST API
- Artificial Intelligence

## Contributing

We welcome contributions from developers, physicians, researchers, and students.
